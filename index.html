Problem Statement

Currently there is almost no communiction between ambulances and hospitals before they arrive with a patient. This causes dead time and inaccuracy leading to fatal results.

Our solution

We are currently designing a communication system that collects patient data and transfers it to hospitals modeled after a Patient Tracking System design. Our system is built to require zero additional infrastructure and enables secure and efficent data transfer.

Future work

We are currently working with two hospitals to implement and refine the system. We plan on using this data to launch an NLP focused analytics platform to facilitate a data based healthcare model.
